Project 3: Experimental Clock

This emotion-based clock visualizes mood through color, shifting from blue (upset) to pink (excited). A rotating hand mimics the second hand, speeding up or slowing down based on your emotional state, reflecting the subjective feeling of time. While it also displays the actual time, the clock serves as a reminder that how we spend time—and the positivity we bring to it—determines its value and meaning.
